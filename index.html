We specialize in orthopaedics

We focus in General Orthopedic Surgery (including fracture and repairs), Sports Medicine and Arthroscopic Surgery, Joint Replacement Surgery, Fractures and Trauma, and Pediatric Orthopedics. We are experts in the following surgeries:

  • Hip Revision Hip Revision surgery provides immediate pain reflief, increased mobility, and improved strength and coodination of your torso and legs.
  • Carpal Tunnel Endoscopic carpal tunnel surgery has a very high rate of success. It is painless, non invasive and has a quick recovery period.
  • Total Shoulder Arthroplasty Endoscopic carpal tunnel surgery has a very high rate of success. It is painless, non invasive and has a quick recovery period.
  • ACL Reconstruction ACL reconstruction surgery lowers the risk post opertive infections, descreases post operative pain, results in faster rehabilitation and lower costs.
  • Anterior Total Hip Arthroplasty Our Anterior Hip Replacement surgery has less damage to major muscels, less post-operative pain, results in faster recover, and decreased risk of hip dislocation.
  • Knee Arthroscopy Knee arthroscopy surgery is minimally invasive and only takes a couple hours to perform. Many patients notice reduced pain from injury or from a arthritic knee.
  • Shoulder Revision Shoulder revision surgery removes pain or instability from past failed surgeries. We have found that many patients have had a poorly executed shoulder surgery, and often times their quality of life can be improved with our procedure.
  • Total Knee Arthroplasty Total knee arthroplasty, or TKR replaces an arthritic knee joint with a artifical joint. It provides relief from sever pain, and stiffness. Additionaly, TKR, can remove bowleg or kncok knee deformities.
  • Shoulder Arthroscopy Shoulder arthrosopy procedures are less invasive and have quicker recovery times that other surgeries. Patients also regain more use of their shoulder with less brusing.

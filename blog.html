ACL Injuries On The Rise In Young Women

A torn ACL is a common injury in athletes and those with active lifestyles, and is on a steady rise in young women in particular. Now more than ever, young female athletes are tearing their ACL’s or re-tearing them after surgery and physical therapy (Approximately 25% of women will retear them
